Higher Education Through TVET, Innovation & Entreprenuership Conference

Conference Theme – The Role of Higher Education through TVET, Innovation and Entrepreneurship in Promoting Industrialisation and Economic Growth

Register

About The Conference

Bulawayo Polytechnic and the University of Venda are pleased to announce their first upcoming Joint International Research Conference to be held under the theme “The Role of Higher Education through TVET, Innovation and Entrepreneurship in Promoting Industrialisation and Economic Growth." The conference aims to bring together academia, industry professionals, policymakers, and other stakeholders to explore and discuss the innovative role of Higher Education, and Entrepreneurship for sustainable development and promotion of Industrialisation and Economic growth.

Conference Objectives:

  • Promote the Integration of Innovation in higher education
  • Strengthen University -TVET - Community- Industry Partnerships
  • Foster Entrepreneurship Ecosystems in Higher Education learning
  • Engage with policymakers on developing frameworks that support the role of tertiary institutions in driving industrialisation, innovation, and entrepreneurship for national development
  • Establish the contribution of tertiary institutions in achieving the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) through innovative education practices, technological advancements, and entrepreneurship

Why attend?

The conference seeks to highlight how higher education and TVET- technical training institutions can be catalysts for economic transformation, fostering job creation, promoting sustainable industries, and developing entrepreneurship ecosystems
